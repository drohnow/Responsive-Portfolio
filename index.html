About Me

David Rohnow makes his home San Diego, California. He has an extensive twenty-year career in IT where he led large scale IT organizations for SaaS (Software-as-a-Service) providers in the fitness and education Industries. In 2014 David decided to moved into an IT Consulting role where he continued to acquire technology knowledge and expertise in Cloud and other next-generation technologies.

Most recently, David has built extensive knowledge in the DevOps area of the technology field. He is also currently enrolled in the UCSD Dev Boot Camp where he is undergoing intense training to become a Full Web Stack Developer.

The combination of IT infrastructure and DevOps skills along with his soon to be newly acquired software development tools will be a value to clients that are interested in modernizing their organization’s tecchnology. Technology modernization will significantly improve end user productivity, responsiveness to business needs (IT Agility), and top IT talent retention which will net greater customer satisfaction.
